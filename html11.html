the best avocado egg salad

description

Avocado Egg Salad – no mayo here! just avocados, eggs, herbs, lemon juice, and
salt. Especially good on an everything bagel. Just saying.


ingredients

tofu

  • 2 avocados
  • 8 eggs
  • a handful of dill
  • juice of one lemon
  • a pinch of salt
  • a drizzle of olive oil (as needed)


instructions

  1. Hard boil the eggs Cover the eggs with water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil,
    turn heat off, cover and rest (on hot burner) for 8-10 minutes. Run under
    cold water and break off the shells. Cut the eggs into small pieces.
  2. Mash the avocados: Mash the avocados in a bowl with the back of a large wooden spoon until mostly smooth.
  3. Mix and serve::Mix the eggs with the avocados, herbs, lemon juice, salt, and olive oil if you need it. Serve immediately at room temperature, or chill and serve cold.

notes

  • I LOVE LOVE LOVE this avocado egg salad on an everything bagel. It’s just dreamy.
